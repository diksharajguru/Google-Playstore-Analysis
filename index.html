AIM

The aim of our analysis is to provide insights about android applications and their categories. We want to deep dive in data for the factors of influences on an application, to know why and how certain applications succeed and others. Also, what is required for an application to be considered as successfully topping the charts.


OBJECTIVES

  • Visualization of Inferences for better understanding
  • Pre-processing of Dataset (Data Cleaning, Data Transformation)
  • To construct a Forecasting Model to predict the Global Android app market
  • To provide insights on app features and the current state of the Android app market
  • To gather and analyze detailed information on apps in the Google Play Store
  • To perform sentiment analysis on app store data

DATASET

The Play Store apps data has enormous potential to drive app-making businesses to success. Actionable insights can be drawn for developers to work on and capture the Android market. The 2 datasets are chosen from Kaggle.i.e, googleplaystore.csv and googleplaystore_user_reviews.csv. googleplaystore.csv is the web scraped data of 10k Play Store apps for analyzing the Android market. It consists of in total of 10841 rows and 13 columns. The columns of the dataset are as follows:

1. App 2. Category
3. Rating 4. Reviews
5. Size 6. Installs
7. Type 8. Price
9. Content Rating 10. Genres
11. Last Updated 12. Current Version
13. Android Version

googleplaystore_user_reviews.csv this dataset consists of around 65k rows and 5 columns. The columns in googleplaystore_user_reviews.csv. are as follows :

1. App 2. Translated review
3. Sentiment polarity 4. Sentiment
5. Sentiment Subjectivity
