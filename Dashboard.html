Data Visualization

Data visualization is the graphical representation of information and data. By using visual elements like charts, graphs, and maps, data visualization tools provide an accessible way to see and understand trends, outliers, and patterns in data

Visuals

Which category has the highest share of (active) apps in the market?


• Game and Communication apps have the highest market prevalence.
• Interestingly, Productivity, Social and Tools apps are also catching up.

Apps ratings across categories


• Almost all app categories perform well.
• Events and education categories have highest average ratings hence they produce highest quality apps.
• Dating and tools categories have lowest average ratings.

Current pricing trend - How to price your app?


• Clearly, Finance, Lifestyle, Medical and Family apps are the most expensive.
• All other apps have average price under 0.5$.
• Surprisingly, all game apps are reasonably averaged below 0.3$.

Ratings for free Vs paid apps


• Though downloads for free apps are more than paid apps but the average ratings for paid apps are more than free apps.
• Hence, we can say that the paid apps are designed and developed to cater to specific functionalities
• Though paid apps are preferred less they offer quality service.

Sizing strategy Light Vs Bulky


• Most top rated apps are optimally sized between ~2MB to ~40MB - neither too light nor too heavy.

Are paid apps downloaded as much as free apps?


• Clearly people prefer free apps over paid apps.

Sentiment Polarity Distribution

free apps receive a lot of harsh comments, as indicated by the outliers on the negative y-axis. Reviews for paid apps appear never to be extremely negative. This may indicate something about app quality, i.e., paid apps being of higher quality than free apps on average. The median polarity score for paid apps is a little higher than free apps, thereby syncing with our previous observation.
